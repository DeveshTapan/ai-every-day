Use AI wisely

Smart tools still need human judgment.

AI is a helpful partner, not a perfect authority. A few good habits make all the difference.

✓

Check important facts

AI can make confident mistakes. Verify medical, legal, financial, and other important information.

◉

Protect your privacy

Avoid sharing passwords, private company data, financial details, or sensitive personal information.

♙

Keep yourself in charge

Use AI to support—not replace—your experience, values, creativity, and final decisions.
